Stegt flæsk - med persillesovs

The Danish national dish

Fried pork with parsley gravy and boiled potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Sliced pork.
  • Potatoes.
  • Parsley.
  • Butter.
  • Milk.
  • Flour.
  • Salt & Peber.
  • Nutmeg.

Steps

  1. Boilt potatoes.
  2. Fry the porkon a pan.
  3. Parsley gravy
    1. Melt butter in a pot.
    2. Add flour & wisk.
    3. Pour in the milk, keep wisking!
    4. Chop and add Parsley.
    5. U better still be wisking!
    6. Add salt, pepper & nutmeg to taste.
  4. Enjoy!
