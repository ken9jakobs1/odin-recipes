Dansih Risengrød

Rice boiled in milk, served with butter, sugar and cinnemon.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • water
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Sugar
  • Cinnemon
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil Rice in a bit of water.
  2. Add Milk.
  3. Cook and stear.
  4. Add vanilla.
  5. Cook till smooth.
  6. Pour on a plate.
  7. Serve with
    • Sugar
    • Cinnemon
    • Butter
  8. Enjoy
